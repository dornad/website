About

I’m a software engineer who moves between two worlds: building production iOS apps used by millions and crafting games and interactive experiences with small, scrappy teams.

Most recently I was the sole iOS engineer at Adapter, an AI assistant startup, where I took the app from zero to the App Store in under a year. Before that, I spent years at Warby Parker building AR and measurement tools that Apple featured.

I hold a Master’s from Carnegie Mellon’s Entertainment Technology Center, where I learned to think at the intersection of technology, storytelling, and design. That perspective shapes everything I build.
