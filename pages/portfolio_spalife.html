Spa Life

Spa Life is a fast-paced Facebook game where players must manage a busy spa and all of its demanding clientele. The goal is keep your clients happy and create the most luxurious spa destination in your city. Spa Life is developed by Manhattan-based social games startup FreshPlanet in partnership with Clarins, a global industry leader spa and skincare expertise.

  • Inside Social Games: http://bit.ly/zo9uPX
  • Mashable: http://on.mash.to/AsZNpM

Among my responsabilites were:

  • Game play systems; chief among them the tutorial section of the game.
  • After launch I worked on maintenance, adding new missions and some missions that promoted the Clarins brand.
  • iPad version of the game, based on Adobe AIR and the Starling framework.
