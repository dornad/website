Hello. I'm Daniel, a Software Engineer/Game Developer. Welcome to my site. I'm from Bogota, Colombia.

I co-founded a web startup in Colombia and later worked at a Marketing Communications company. After these two endeavors, I finally embarked on the life-long adventure of Game Development, with a masters degree in Entertainment Technology in the Entertainment Technology Center at Carnegie Mellon University.

During the masters degree, I supported gameplay and interaction in 5 virtual worlds, developed prototypes for three games and developed tools for game sketching and virtual puppeteering.

After finishing my degree at Carnegie Mellon, I joined FreshPlanet in New York City, working on several projects, including SongPop, one of the most successful mobile games of 2012.
