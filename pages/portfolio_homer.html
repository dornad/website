Learn With Homer

Learn With Homer is a children's application that aims to teach children ages 3-6 to read in a very fun and didactic way.

  • Download it here: https://goo.gl/m7D9S3
  • LearnWithHomer has earned several awards, such as Appy Award for Best Educational game, Mom's Choice Gold Award, and it has been featured by NBC, The New York Times and the Today Show.

Among my responsabilites were:

  • Maintenance and improvement of the existing codebase (Objective-C), plus development of new features currently live on the app, including third party SDK integrations.
  • One man tech team (development and engineering, under the CTO)
  • Maintained a hybrid Cocos2D/UIKit/CoreAnimation application.
  • Third Party Library Integration, including writing PodSpecs for new or outdated libraries.
