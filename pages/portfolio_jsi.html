JustSingIt

JustSingIt is a mobile Karaoke/Social Network mashup app. Allowing users to sing, be discovered and earn rewards along the way.

  • Download it here: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/just-sing-it-karaoke-singing/id542468402?mt=8
  • Within only a month of its launch, the app was featured on Business Insider, TechCrunch, NBC; and garnered more than 1,000,000 songs on the platform with an overall 5 star rating in the App Store

Among my responsabilites were:

  • 1 man tech team, after the original developer left. (development and engineering on Mobile, responding directly to the cofounders)
  • Implemented solutions for sharing video, based on AVVideo.
  • Third Party Library Integration via CocoaPods.
