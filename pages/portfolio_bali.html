Balli Plastici

Balli Plastici was a summer long project in which I was the lead programmer for a project that sought to lay the building blocks for a reimagination of Fortunato Depero's Futurist puppetry ballet for the digital age.

My work, together with Oscar Ramirez and Goutham Dindukurthi was the essential building block of a successful ETC semester project, Depero Futuristi, which has been presented in many venues around the world.

Features

This project focused on the following

  • Manipulation of models in a puppet-like behaviour.
  • Able to define stages and scenes, following Depero's performance.
  • Extensible and robust framework that could be used by any non-technical puppeteer

Results

Our work was the foundation block for Depero Fututisti, a semester project at Carnegie Mellon, which has been recognized and exposed in various venues around the world. Visit their website
